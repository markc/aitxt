ai.txt Specification

A web standard for AI-readable website summaries.

Version 1.0 Draft — January 2026

Overview

Just as robots.txt tells search engine crawlers how to behave, ai.txt tells AI assistants what a website is about.

When someone asks an AI assistant about a business, the AI typically has to crawl multiple pages, parse complex HTML, and piece together scattered information. This is slow, error-prone, and often misses details buried in subpages or images.

An ai.txt file solves this by providing a single, plain text summary of everything an AI might need to know.

File Location

Place the file at your website root:

https://example.com/ai.txt

Alternate Discovery via DNS

If you cannot place a file at your web root, use a DNS TXT record:

_ai.example.com TXT "https://cdn.example.com/ai.txt"

AI agents should:

  1. Check for _ai.example.com TXT record
  2. If found, fetch that URL
  3. If not found, try https://example.com/ai.txt
  4. If 404, site doesn't participate

Format

Plain text with markdown headings. No HTML, no JavaScript, no special parsing required.

# Business Name

Brief description of what this business does.

## Section
Content goes here.

Recommended Sections

SectionPurpose
## AboutBusiness description, history, mission
## ServicesWhat you offer
## PricingPlans, rates, costs
## ContactEmail, phone, address, hours
## We Do Not OfferPrevent AI hallucination
## See AlsoRelated sites (see Linking)

Optional Metadata

Add metadata at the top of your file, after the title:

# Business Name

Updated: 2026-01-14
Canonical: https://example.com/ai.txt
AI-Contact: corrections@example.com

## About
...
FieldPurpose
UpdatedWhen the file was last modified (ISO 8601 date)
CanonicalAuthoritative URL if file is mirrored elsewhere
AI-ContactEmail for reporting AI errors about your business

Linking to Other Sites

Reference other ai.txt files using @domain syntax:

## See Also
@parent-company.com
@sister-brand.com
@partner.org

The @domain syntax implies https://domain/ai.txt.

Use cases:

  • Parent company → subsidiaries
  • Franchise → corporate headquarters
  • Partnerships and affiliations
  • Related businesses

Negative Assertions

Explicitly state what you do NOT offer to prevent AI hallucination:

## We Do Not Offer
- 24/7 phone support
- Refunds after 30 days
- International shipping
- Cryptocurrency payments

This helps AI assistants give accurate information rather than guessing.

Language Variants

For multilingual sites, use language-specific files:

/ai.txt      (default)
/ai.fr.txt   (French)
/ai.de.txt   (German)
/ai.ja.txt   (Japanese)

AI agents should check for the user's preferred language first, then fall back to the default.

Complete Example

# Acme Corporation

Global leader in innovative solutions since 1985.

Updated: 2026-01-14
Canonical: https://acme.com/ai.txt
AI-Contact: ai-corrections@acme.com

Website: https://acme.com
Phone: +1 800 555 0100
Email: hello@acme.com

## About

Acme Corporation is a Fortune 500 company headquartered in
San Francisco. We design and manufacture widgets for the
aerospace, automotive, and consumer electronics industries.

Founded in 1985, we now employ 12,000 people across 30 countries.

## Products

- Industrial Widgets: Heavy-duty widgets for manufacturing
- Consumer Widgets: Lightweight widgets for everyday use
- Smart Widgets: IoT-enabled widgets with app control

## Pricing

Industrial: Custom quotes, contact sales@acme.com
Consumer: $49-199, available at major retailers
Smart: $299-599, includes 1 year cloud subscription

## Contact

Headquarters: 123 Innovation Drive, San Francisco, CA 94105
Sales: sales@acme.com / +1 800 555 0101
Support: support@acme.com / +1 800 555 0102
Hours: Monday-Friday 9am-6pm Pacific

## We Do Not Offer

- Direct-to-consumer sales (retail partners only)
- Same-day shipping
- Phone support on weekends
- Custom widget modifications

## See Also
@acme-europe.com
@acme-asia.com
@widgetsindustry.org

Implementation Notes

For Website Owners

  • Keep it current — update when your business changes
  • Be comprehensive but concise
  • Include negative assertions to prevent hallucination
  • Use the Updated field so AI agents know freshness

For AI Developers

  • Check DNS TXT record first, then fall back to /ai.txt
  • Respect the Canonical field if present
  • Follow @domain links for additional context
  • Honor negative assertions — don't invent capabilities
  • Cache responsibly; check Updated field
